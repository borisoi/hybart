Temporary installation by the construction site of the [Cricoteka](https://www.cricoteka.pl/pl/en/) museum, staged as its first museum space open to the public. Part of the [ArtBoom festival of public art, Krakow, Poland](http://www.artboomfestival.pl/en/80/3/88/Boris-Oicherman-curator-lukasz-Bialkowski). Curator: Łukasz Białkowski. Mirrors, steel pipes, plywood.
