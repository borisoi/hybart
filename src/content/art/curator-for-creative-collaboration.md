See [this document](/s/Oicherman-Boris-Curatorial-Projects.pdf) for the description of five selected projects I curated and [this interview](https://mnartists.walkerart.org/institution-as-living-artist-as-can-opener-as-system-change) for a brief summary of my work there. .
