Lights installed in the abandoned building flash a morse-coded text, the ending of the [parable of Rabbi Baal Shem-Tov](https://jewishcamp.org/wp-content/uploads/2017/05/Cmon-Baby-Story-2.pdf):

I *am unable to light the fire, and I do not know the prayer, and I cannot even find the place in the forest. All I can do is tell the story, and this must be sufficient. And it was sufficient*

In collaboration with Vienne Chan. Incandescent lamps, cables, controller. December 2014, Tel Aviv
