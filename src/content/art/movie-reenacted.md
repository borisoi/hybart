Performance. 3.5-hour-long live reenactment of Bill Bollinger’s moving image piece “Movie” (1970) by 70 performers. Performers are instructed to try to balance the log on its end. Logs were sourced in the local forest and donated to the school nearby afterwards. June 2015, Hanstholm, Denmark
