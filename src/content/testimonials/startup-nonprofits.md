Boris Oicherman's strategic planning approach has been transformative for Rust Belt Fibershed as we work to revitalize our local textile ecosystem. He introduced the concept of our organization as both a knowledge hub and service provider, giving us a clear structure for prioritizing activities and communicating our value to stakeholders.

Boris guided us in aligning initiatives with this dual role and provided valuable board development insights. He consistently challenged us to elevate our performance, helping us achieve in months what might have taken a year. His approach to fundraising and community engagement, including a creative tiered strategy, has given us new confidence in our efforts.

Throughout our work, Boris balanced big-picture thinking with practical steps. His guidance has been instrumental in positioning us as a catalyst for change, helping weave together sustainability, economic development, and cultural heritage into a robust strategy for rebuilding our local textile ecosystem. Thanks to his blend of strategic acumen and creative problem-solving, we're better equipped to fulfill our mission of fostering a sustainable, local textile economy. Working with Boris has truly elevated our organization's potential and impact, accelerating our progress in ways we never thought possible.
