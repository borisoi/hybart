Boris is a rare combination of visionary and pragmatist, adept at synthesizing diverse concepts into coherent strategies and actionable plans. His talent lies in harmonizing competing forces and fostering collaboration among diverse stakeholders to achieve constructive outcomes.

This unique skill set equips Boris to develop an exceptional strategy for place-based programs at the Foundry Arts, a new groundbreaking cultural institution in Cleveland. His contributions to the plan for this 80,000-square-foot facility helped us chart a way forward that transcends traditional arts programming, addressing the cultural, economic, and social impacts on long-overlooked communities through an innovative three-pronged approach: community services, support for cultural organizations, and resources for artists.

Boris's multifaceted strategy for the Foundry Arts demonstrates the arc of his ability to create models deeply rooted in local needs while drawing on global best practices. His work enhances the institution's relevance and likelihood of success in today's rapidly evolving, competitive cultural landscape, potentially serving as an inspiration for similar initiatives nationwide.
